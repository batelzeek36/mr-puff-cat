| Item | Source | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Revolution Plus (preferred) or Bravecto Topical | VET | Vet-only Rx. Revolution Plus is the lower-risk pick, see product-notes.md |
| Broad-spectrum dewormer | VET | Especially if flea-positive (tapeworm risk) |

If the vet visit slips a few days and Mr. Puff is visibly scratching:
- **Advantage II Large Cat (single dose)** at PETCO-H or Amazon as a stopgap. Apply between the shoulder blades. Don't double up if you also plan to do Revolution Plus shortly after, space them by ~2 weeks.

## ENVIRONMENTAL FLEA TREATMENT

The active ingredient you want is **(s)-methoprene** or **pyriproxyfen** (the "IGR" - insect growth regulator). The IGR is what stops eggs from hatching for months. Plain pyrethrin sprays without an IGR only kill adults and leave you re-treating every 2 weeks.

| Item | Source | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Adams Flea & Tick Carpet & Home Spray, 16 oz** | WALMART-H, PETCO-H | (s)-methoprene 0.09% + pyrethrins. 7-month residual. **First choice.** |
| Adams Flea & Tick Home Spray, 24 oz trigger | WALMART-H | Same chemistry, bigger bottle, easier to spray crevices |
| Black Flag 1 gal RTU "Plus Growth Regulator" | HD-H | Good if you want to soak baseboards / lanai. Has the IGR. |
| Black Flag Yard Concentrate (32 oz hose-end) | HD-H | Lambda-cyhalothrin + pyriproxyfen 0.125%. **Outdoor only**, treats yard up to 5,000 sqft. Useful if she sleeps under your house. |

**Skip:** plain "Raid Flea Killer" without "Plus" (no IGR), any spray labeled only as "kills on contact."

## SKIP / AVOID (do not buy these by mistake)

- **Anything with permethrin** in the cat-applied section. Permethrin is fine on dogs, lethal to cats. Read the back of every flea product.
- **Hartz / Sergeant's spot-on flea treatments.** Long history of cat injuries.
- **Plain Raid Flea Killer** without "Plus" (no IGR, you'll be re-treating monthly).
- **Flea collars.** Mostly ineffective, some toxic. The Seresto collar is the only one with real data and even that's contested.
- **Alcohol wipes for paw cleaning.** Drying, irritating, unnecessary.

## One-trip routes

**Pahoa-only run (today, while waiting on vet callback):**
- LONGS-P: hydrocortisone, Benadryl/Zyrtec, hot-water detergent if you need it
- ACE-P: vacuum bags
- MALAMA-P: dedicated washcloth, blanket, anything LONGS missed

**Hilo run (one trip, after vet appointment is set):**
- WALMART-H: Adams Flea & Tick Carpet & Home Spray, stainless bowls if buying
- HD-H: Black Flag yard concentrate (only if she's denning under the house)
- PETCO-H: flea comb, paw wipes, carrier if not borrowing, Advantage II as Rx-stopgap if needed

The Hilo Walmart, Home Depot, Target, and Petco are all within a few blocks of each other off Hwy 11 and Makaala / Puainako, so you can hit them in one loop.
